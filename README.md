# 🧠 Synapse

AI-Powered Healthcare Assistant 🚀  
Developed during **Aavishkar Pravah 2.0 Hackathon**

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Synapse is an intelligent AI-powered healthcare platform designed to assist in patient healthcare analysis, medical report understanding, and contextual healthcare interaction using Generative AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

The project creates a smart connection between patients, healthcare data, and Artificial Intelligence — inspired by the concept of a biological synapse in the human brain.

---

# 🔍 Problem Statement

Traditional healthcare systems often struggle with:
- Managing patient medical history efficiently
- Understanding complex medical reports
- Providing intelligent contextual healthcare assistance
- Quick retrieval of important patient insights

Synapse solves this problem using AI-powered semantic retrieval and contextual medical assistance.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

Synapse uses:
- Generative AI
- Vector Databases
- RAG Architecture
- Embedding-based Retrieval
- AI-powered Chatbot Assistance

to provide personalized healthcare interaction and intelligent patient data analysis.

Semantic Retrieval

---

# 🤖 GPT Model & AI Training

Synapse integrates a custom AI chatbot trained on patient healthcare datasets and medical history records.

The chatbot is capable of:
- Understanding patient conditions
- Answering healthcare-related queries
- Retrieving contextual medical information
- Providing intelligent AI-based healthcare assistance

The AI system uses:
- Groq LLM
- HuggingFace Embeddings
- LangChain RAG Pipeline
- Chroma Vector Database

to deliver context-aware responses from patient medical records.
